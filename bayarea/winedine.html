Eat Around the Bay Area

One of the best activities to do in the Bay Area is to eat eat eat and drink drink drink. Home of some of the best cuisines in the world, this region of California has one of the most diverse palates. Whether you're looking for Italian, French, Chinese or the more obscure dishes like Burmese curry, or the cheapest tacos, the cities in the Bay Area is guaranteed to satisfy your taste buds. If you are looking to drink, Napa is your bet. Known for its regional wine, this gem is comparable to France!
