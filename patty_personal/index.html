Places to eat in NYC

  • Breakfast
  • Lunch
  • Dinner

Breakfast

Clinton Street Baking Company
Friedmans
Feast

Breakfast is one of the most important meals of the day. When visiting NYC be sure to check out Clinton Street Bakery for their delicious pastry, Friedman's for the amazing fried chicken and waffles, and Feast for its amazing multi-coursed brunch menu!

Lunch

Black Iron Burger
Xi'an Famous Foods
The Picnic Basket

Next up we have lunch! There are so many amazing lunch options when visiting the Big Apple. Make sure to check out Black Iron Burger for their amazing truffle burger with a side of garlic fries, Xi'an's homemade hand-pulled noodles, and The Picnic Basket's wonderful sandwiches - they're all great!

Dinner

Toloache
Sushi Yasaka
Osteria Morini

Last but not least, the best meal of the day has to be dinner. The city that never sleeps has an endless selection of different cuisines to offer. If you are craving Mexican, be sure to check out Toloache in midtown west. If you want seafood, Sushi Yasaka has the freshest fish and cheapest omakase set. If you are feeling carbs for the night, be sure to checkout Osteria Morini's pasta - FYI: they have $10 pastas after 9pm on Monday nights!
